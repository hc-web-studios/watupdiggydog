Our Story

Based in the heart of Everton Park, Watupdiggydog Dog Wash and Groomer is a local business dedicated to keeping your canine companions looking and feeling their best. This friendly, neighbourhood establishment offers a range of services designed to pamper your pooch, from a simple wash and dry to full-service grooming sessions.

Watupdiggydog prides itself on its caring and professional approach. The team understands that every dog is unique, and they work to create a comfortable and stress-free experience for each furry client. Whether your dog needs a quick tidy-up or a breed-specific haircut, they use high-quality products and gentle techniques to ensure a positive visit.

The business has become a go-to for many Everton Park residents who value a personal touch and reliable service. With a focus on community and canine welfare, Watupdiggydog Dog Wash and Groomer is more than just a place to get your dog cleaned—it's a trusted local spot where four-legged friends are treated like family.
