Our Services

Oodles Poodles & Doodles

A comprehensive pet cut including clipping and scissor finishing — a practical, neat style for most companion dogs. Precision trimming to keep your pup comfortable and looking smart.

Service Overview

What to expect

Our Full Groom — Pet Cut is tailored for companion dogs and focuses on comfort, hygiene and a tidy finished look. Sessions include a consultation, gentle handling, and finishing touches to suit your dog's lifestyle.

What's included

  • • Full body clip or scissor finish
  • • Shampoo + conditioning
  • • Nail trim & grind
  • • Ear check & clean
  • • Paw pad tidy
  • • Light cologne and finishing brush

Duration & care

Most full grooms take between 90–150 minutes depending on coat, temperament and any required de-matting. We recommend scheduling morning appointments for calmer experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our Full Groom service.

How long will the groom take?
Typical sessions last 90–150 minutes depending on coat and behaviour. We always aim for calm, stress-free appointments.
Do you handle nervous dogs?
Yes — we use gentle handling techniques and short breaks. For very anxious dogs we recommend a Puppy First Groom introduction course.
Can I book recurring appointments?
Absolutely. Ask at the time of booking or contact us to set up a regular schedule for maintenance grooms.
